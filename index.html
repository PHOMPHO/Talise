Introduction

Alive since an era way before Meridell was founded or even given this name, Talise is a chaotic draik that wants and cares about nothing more than to hoard gold and precious objects by any means necessary, be it pillaging the enclosing small villages or ending someone else, nothing is too iniquitous for this vile and ancient creature. Living mostly hidden in her cave located deep inside the forests that surround the land of the Kingdom Meridell, she keeps her loot away from the preying eyes of others, however, her many feats of atrocity have generated many legends since the olden days, moving many courageous and greedy adventures alike to try and steal away her fortune without success, at this moment their bones are pilled in a throne formation at the back of her cave.

Personality

Talise is an entitled woman who believes all the riches of the world were created by the higher powers only for her while others should be crawling at her feet. With the moral code worse than of a thief she prefers to keep her pillaging and red baths inside the boarders of the Kingdom of Meridell, believing it to be the most fun place to be and with no plans of moving her antics elsewhere. With her only source of happiness being the misery of others and her treasure, most of her emotions are exaggerated and sarcastic towards others, in the very rare occasions were she feels threatened or stressed her default state is to mock her opponent while trying to cover the shakiness in her voice and the scared facial movements. Always bearing a sadistic smile on her face she goes about life in a very destructive manner.

World

Neopia is a planet in the Goldilocks Zone of an unknown star system, at an orbital distance to produce years of 365.25 days (equivalent to Earth). It has a day/night cycle that is 24 hours in length. It supports a wide array of life forms, many species of which collectively are grouped together as Neopets. Home to many different kinds of creatures, including Neopets and Faeries, Neopia is quite abundant with resources. This world is divided into two main continents. This once calm and ordinary world has seen it's fair share of wars and red baths.
